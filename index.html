Our Heritage

Crafting Memories Since 1987

Founded in the heart of Grasse, France, Noir Essence began as a small artisan perfume house dedicated to the ancient art of fragrance creation. Our master perfumers travel the globe sourcing the rarest ingredients.

Each fragrance in our collection is meticulously composed over months, sometimes years, ensuring every note harmonizes to create an unforgettable olfactory experience.

We believe that a great perfume is more than a scent — it's an invisible accessory that completes who you are.
